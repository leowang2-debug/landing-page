Business & Financial Highlights

How NoteWave creates value for students, universities, and investors.

Revenue Model: Freemium

Free Tier

  • 180 minutes of transcription per month
  • Basic summaries only
  • .txt export only
  • Ad-supported experience

Premium Tier

€5/month or €50/year (save 2 months)

  • Unlimited transcription
  • Advanced AI summaries + keyword extraction
  • No ads
  • Export to PDF and DOCX

University Partnerships

  • Under 5,000 students: €8,000/year
  • 5,000 to 15,000 students: €15,000/year
  • Over 15,000 students: €25,000/year
  • LMS integration (Moodle, Canvas etc.)

Additional Revenue: Advertising

Free users generate about €0.20 per month from banner ads and rewarded video ads ("Watch an ad for 30 extra minutes").
