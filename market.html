Target Audience

Who We Serve

Our primary audience is English-speaking university students who want to simplify their academic workload. These users struggle to listen, understand, and write notes simultaneously in fast-paced lectures.

Accuracy

Highly accurate speech-to-text transcription is essential for capturing lectures correctly.

Speed

Real-time transcription with minimal delay is critical for usability during lectures.

Structure

Students prefer bullet-point summaries and well-organised notes for revision.

Market Size

Including international English-taught programmes, the total market expands to approximately 30–50 million students globally, representing a significant growth opportunity.

Market Trends

Growing Global Demand

English-taught university programmes have grown by 22% between 2021–2024, indicating a rapidly expanding global audience. This trend shows increasing demand for tools that support English-based learning environments worldwide.

Competition

Competitive Landscape

Direct Competitors

  • Otter.ai: Strong transcription but focused on business meetings.
  • Other AI note tools exist but lack academic specialization.

Indirect Competitors

  • Notion / OneNote: Require manual note-taking.
  • Google / Microsoft: Large ecosystems but not student-focused.

NoteWave differentiates itself by focusing specifically on lecture environments, offering structured summaries, speaker separation, and student-friendly pricing.

Challenges

Market Entry Barriers

  • Technology: High accuracy AI transcription requires advanced systems.
  • Brand Trust: Established competitors already dominate the space.
  • Financial: Cloud storage and AI processing create ongoing costs.
  • Distribution: Reaching students requires marketing and partnerships.
