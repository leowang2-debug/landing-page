Market & Customers

Target Audience

English-speaking university students globally. The Total Addressable Market (TAM) is estimated at roughly 30-50 million students.

User Persona

Our core users are students who struggle to listen, comprehend, and write simultaneously. This includes students dealing with fast-paced lecturers, strong accents, or complex technical subjects where missing a beat means falling behind.

Why Choose Us (Competitor Comparison)

NoteWave stands out because it is purpose-built for academia.

  • Unlike Otter.ai: Which is built and optimized for business meetings and corporate dialogue, NoteWave understands academic structures and terminology.
  • Unlike Notion or OneNote: Which rely on manual text input and user organization, NoteWave automates the capture and structuring process entirely.

We offer specialized features like advanced speaker separation in lecture halls, automated generation of structured academic summaries (flashcards, key points), all at a highly accessible price point tailored for student budgets.
