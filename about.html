Problem & Solution

The Friction

The Challenge

Take comprehensive notes that you can use for revision by learning to use the note-taking process that suits your learning style. Many students are not able to manage the speed and complexity of a lecturer, especially in big classrooms or where the audio is not clear, and therefore will only end up with fragmented and poor-quality notes.

Our Solution

NoteWave is a leading-edge learning platform that leverages AI technology to digitally record lectures and automatically generate real-time transcripts, intelligently summaries and rephrase learning notes. With NoteWave, students can learn much faster without having to spend too much time on writing notes.

Product Innovation

Core Innovation

Value Proposition for NoteWave: Our target Value Proposition for NoteWave is for large lecture classes. We want to ensure students are engaged in the lecture and understanding the content, and at the same time minimize the amount of cognitive load required to note the content being communicated by the Lecturer.

⚡ Real-Time Transcription

Capture lecture content as it happens with near real-time processing.

📝 Structured Summaries

Convert raw transcription into usable academic notes and key points.

🎓 Lecture-Focused Design

Built specifically for large classes, strong accents, and noisy rooms.

How It Works

The enhanced version, NoteWave, utilizes sophisticated AI that provides fast, close to real-time, highly accurate transcription and fully structured learning materials.

  • Frontend: React Library is very small, resulting in a very fast, simple and responsive user interface on all devices.
  • Backend: Python, handling robust data processing, audio streaming, and API integrations securely.
  • The AI that powers SmartNotes: We use the OpenAI Whisper API to give you top-notch transcription, even with heavy academic jargon and strong accents - all in real time.

Who It Serves

Target Audience

NoteWave is an app for university students attending large lectures who need an accurate, real-time transcription and set of notes. It can be a very useful tool for international students and students who need other assistive learning tools.

Business Model

Revenue Model

NoteWave is a free, open-source note-taking app in development, with the first release planned for Q2 2028. Basic Transcription Features are free. Suppose you want the advanced Summary features, the ability to store an unlimited number of Audio records, as well as to Export your Transcription as PDF, Word or as a mediafile export, e.g. as an audiobook, that will be €5 per month. In addition, there will also be advertisements, which should bring further revenue. We are also planning to partner up with several universities.
